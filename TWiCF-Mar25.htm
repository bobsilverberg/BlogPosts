The This Week in ColdFusion Podcast is Back! That's right, the mighty fine folks who bring you the This Week in ColdFusion podcast have released a new episode. After what seems like an interminable hiatus, the boys are back and guess what? I'm on it too! I was interviewed about the upcoming cf.Objective() conference and the incredible pre-conference training that will be available. I also discussed the Pecha Kucha BOF that I am organizing.

After my cameo Brian and Mike had a lively and informative chat about design patterns, which I stuck my nose into from time to time. It was great fun and I encourage you to give it a listen. It's available via iTunes, and can also be listened to or downloaded directly from the show page.
